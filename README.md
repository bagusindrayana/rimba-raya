## Rimba Raya
From a single sapling to an endless jungle—watch your world scale up!

Welcome to Rimba Raya ("The Great Forest"), Start with nothing but empty soil and a single seed. Plant it, nurture it, and witness the satisfying magic of nature as it physically scales up right before your eyes.  Watch your forest grow and expand and see the forest animals arrive!

### Gameplay :
Once a tree reaches its maximum scale, it rewards you with Growth Points. These points are your currency to expand your paradise:

Plant new tree to expand your forest and get more Growth Points.
Place unique objects to decorate your woodland scene and attract animals!.

### Simple Control :
- mouse left click to place object
- mouse right click to remove object
- middle mouse to rotate camera
